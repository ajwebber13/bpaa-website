Brother Charles M. Ladd F'96

Board Chair | Beta Phi Alumni Association

Biography

Charles M. Ladd, Fall 1996 initiate, is an experienced business development and marketing professional in the natural gas and crude oil sectors. He has over 25 years of experience in the energy industry, specializing in commercial contracts, asset development, and mergers and acquisitions with major global energy companies.

He currently manages and budgets assets generating over $300 million in gross revenue and is responsible for developing five-year rolling forecasts and growth strategies.
