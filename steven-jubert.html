Dr. Steven B. Jubert, Ed.D

Board Member

Dr. Steven Jubert is a native of Lake Charles, Louisiana and a proud initiate of the Beta Phi Chapter of Alpha Phi Alpha Fraternity, Inc., initiated in 1999 as part of “The Great Revolt” line.

He holds a Doctorate of Education (Ed.D) and has over 20 years of senior leadership experience in higher education.

He currently serves as Associate Vice President for Student Living, Learning, and Engagement at Widener University.
