Brother Steven B. Jubert, Jr., Ed.D

Dr. Steven Jubert is a native of Lake Charles, Louisiana and a proud initiate of the Beta Phi Chapter of Alpha Phi Alpha Fraternity, Inc., initiated on November 21, 1999, as part of the jewel line “The Great Revolt.” He brings over 20 years of senior executive leadership in higher education and holds a Doctorate of Education (Ed.D.). He currently serves as Associate Vice President for Student Living, Learning, and Engagement at Widener University in Chester, Pennsylvania.
