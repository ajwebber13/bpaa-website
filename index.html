Our Leadership

The brothers guiding Beta Phi Alumni Association with honor, vision, and dedication to our shared legacy.

Board Chair
Beta Phi Alumni Association
Bro. Charles M. Ladd

As Board Chair, Brother Ladd provides visionary leadership for the Beta Phi Alumni Association, stewarding the chapter’s legacy while advancing its mission of brotherhood, service, and support for Dillard University. His commitment to Alpha Phi Alpha’s founding ideals guides the Association’s direction and growth.

Bro. Charles M. Ladd is an energy sector executive with experience managing major commercial contracts and multimillion-dollar portfolios. His leadership brings strategic discipline, business insight, and a strong commitment to excellence to the Association.
Board Treasurer
Bro. Kael Saloy

Brother Saloy oversees the financial stewardship of the Association, ensuring transparency, accountability, and the responsible management of dues and chapter resources in service of our brothers and Dillard University.

Board Secretary
Bro. Malik B. Bartholomew

Brother Bartholomew maintains the official records, communications, and documentation of the Association, ensuring operational clarity and continuity across all chapter activities and board decisions.

Board Member
Bro. Lester J. McKee, Jr.

As a Golden Brother representative, Brother McKee brings decades of wisdom and lived Alpha experience to the board, bridging the chapter’s storied past with its present leadership and future vision.

Board Member
Bro. Marion B. Bracy

Brother Bracy contributes strategic insight and community-rooted perspective to the board, supporting initiatives that strengthen brotherhood and amplify the Association’s impact throughout the New Orleans region.

Bro. Marion B. Bracy is a lifelong servant leader initiated into Alpha Phi Alpha on November 20, 1981, through Beta Phi Chapter at Dillard University. He has served extensively at the city, state, regional, and national levels of the fraternity, including key appointments to election, awards, and college brother committees, and later became the youngest elected President of Sigma Lambda Chapter. His distinguished career in higher education includes leadership roles at Dillard University and Xavier University of Louisiana, where he helped guide major capital, facilities, and recovery efforts. His professional and fraternity service reflect a deep commitment to excellence, equity, and community impact.
Board Member
Bro. Dr. Steven B. Jubert, Jr.

Dr. Jubert brings academic distinction and professional excellence to the board, exemplifying the scholarship and character at the heart of Alpha Phi Alpha’s mission and Beta Phi’s proud tradition.

Dr. Steven B. Jubert, Jr. is a native of Lake Charles, Louisiana and a proud initiate of Beta Phi Chapter. He serves in higher education leadership and brings extensive experience in student support, institutional operations, and campus advancement. His work reflects a strong commitment to scholarship, leadership, and the development of future generations of students and brothers.
Board Member
Bro. Montae Richardson

Brother Richardson supports the Association with technology leadership, energy, and a forward-thinking approach to growth, helping strengthen the chapter’s digital and organizational future.
