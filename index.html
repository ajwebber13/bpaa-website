About Beta Phi

A legacy rooted in excellence since November 11, 1938.

The Beta Phi Chapter of Alpha Phi Alpha Fraternity, Incorporated was chartered on November 11, 1938, on the campus of Dillard University in New Orleans, Louisiana by eight visionary young men dedicated to scholarship, brotherhood, leadership, and service.

Since its inception, Beta Phi brothers have served in numerous leadership roles on campus and throughout Alpha Phi Alpha Fraternity, Inc., while also maintaining excellence academically and professionally.

Beta Phi has produced leaders on the national, regional, district, and local levels of Alpha Phi Alpha Fraternity, Inc., including regional vice presidents, convention leaders, and the 34th General President, Brother Mark S. Tillman.

The chapter has earned countless honors including:

  • 1985 National Chapter of the Year
  • 2003 Southwestern Region Chapter of the Year
  • National Highest GPA Chapter Award (2021)
  • Multiple Louisiana District awards in scholarship and leadership

At the 85th Anniversary celebration in 2023, the college and alumni brothers officially established the Beta Phi Alumni Association to continue strengthening the bond between alumni brothers, Beta Phi Chapter, and Dillard University.
