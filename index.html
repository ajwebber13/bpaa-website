Beta Phi Alumni Association

The Beta Phi Chapter of Alpha Phi Alpha Fraternity, Incorporated is an entity steeped in the rich traditions of brotherhood, scholarship, leadership, and service. Chartered on November 11, 1938 at Dillard University in New Orleans, Louisiana, by eight courageous men, the chapter has grown into a legacy of excellence.

In 2023, during the 85th Anniversary celebration, the Beta Phi Alumni Association was officially established to support the chapter, its brothers, and Dillard University while promoting brotherhood, leadership, and service.
