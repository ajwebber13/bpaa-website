About Beta Phi

The Beta Phi Chapter of Alpha Phi Alpha Fraternity, Incorporated was chartered on November 11, 1938, at Dillard University in New Orleans, Louisiana. Since then, the chapter has remained rooted in scholarship, leadership, service, and brotherhood.

During the 85th Anniversary Brotherhood Meeting in 2023, the Beta Phi Alumni Association was established to support the chapter, its alumni, and the mission of Dillard University. The Association continues to help build a stronger bridge between the undergraduate brothers and the alumni network.
