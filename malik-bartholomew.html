Biography

Brother Malik B. Bartholomew is a Fall 2002 initiate of the Beta Phi Chapter of Alpha Phi Alpha Fraternity, Inc. He is a historian, photographer, archivist, and entrepreneur dedicated to preserving and sharing cultural history.

He currently serves as the CEO of Know NOLA Tours, LLC, where he leads historical tours, research initiatives, presentations, and consulting services focused on New Orleans history and cultural preservation.

Brother Bartholomew serves on multiple boards throughout New Orleans and has played a key leadership role in fraternity service, including serving as Chair of the Beta Phi Chapter 85th Anniversary Committee.

He is an active member of the Sigma Lambda Chapter of Alpha Phi Alpha Fraternity, Inc. and a Life Member, continuing his commitment to brotherhood, service, and historical preservation.
