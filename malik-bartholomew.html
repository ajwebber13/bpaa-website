Brother Malik B. Bartholomew

Malik Bartholomew is a Fall 2002 initiate of the Beta Phi Chapter of Alpha Phi Alpha Fraternity Inc. Malik is a historian, photographer, archivist, and business owner. He is the CEO of Know NOLA Tours, LLC, where he provides historical tours, research, presentations, and consulting services. He serves on several boards in New Orleans and previously served as Chair of the Beta Phi Chapter 85th Anniversary Committee. He is an active member of the Sigma Lambda Chapter and a Life Member of Alpha Phi Alpha Fraternity, Inc.
