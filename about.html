Our Legacy

The story of Beta Phi Chapter and the foundation of the Beta Phi Alumni Association.

Beta Phi Chapter History

The Beta Phi Chapter of Alpha Phi Alpha Fraternity, Incorporated was chartered on November 11, 1938, by eight founding brothers at Dillard University in New Orleans, Louisiana.

Founding Brothers:

  • Robert Bonner
  • Willard Dumas
  • James L. Hall
  • Major Tillman James Howard
  • Marcus Neusadter Jr.
  • Mahloon Clifton Rhaney
  • Dr. Mitchell W. Spellman
  • Vernon L. Winslow

Milestones & Excellence

1938

Beta Phi Chapter is officially chartered at Dillard University.

1985

Chapter of the Year at the General Convention in Atlanta, Georgia.

2003

Southwestern Region Chapter of the Year under Bro. Kyle Jones.

2013

First endowed scholarship established at Dillard University.

2021

National recognition for highest GPA among Alpha Phi Alpha chapters.

2023

Beta Phi Alumni Association officially established during the 85th Anniversary.

Beta Phi Alumni Association

At the 85th Anniversary of the Beta Phi Chapter, the college and alumni brothers voted to establish the Beta Phi Alumni Association.

The mission of the Beta Phi Alumni Association, Inc. is to encourage and promote the highest ideals of brotherhood, leadership, and service while supporting the Beta Phi Chapter and Dillard University.
