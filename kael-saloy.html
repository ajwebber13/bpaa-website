Biography

Brother Kael Saloy is a Fall 2006 initiate of the Beta Phi Chapter of Alpha Phi Alpha Fraternity, Inc. He currently serves as Chief Financial Officer of ReNew Schools, where he oversees financial strategy, budgeting, and operational growth initiatives.

He earned his Bachelor’s degree in Economics and Finance from Dillard University in 2008. During his time at Dillard, he made history as the first elected Mister Dillard University (2007–2008), representing leadership, excellence, and campus engagement.

Brother Saloy is also an active member of the Sigma Lambda Chapter of Alpha Phi Alpha Fraternity, Inc., continuing his commitment to service, leadership, and brotherhood.
