Brother Kael Saloy

Kael Saloy is a Fall 2006 initiate of the Beta Phi Chapter of Alpha Phi Alpha Fraternity, Inc. Kael serves as Chief Financial Officer of ReNew Schools. He earned a degree in Economics and Finance from Dillard University in 2008 and was the first elected Mister Dillard University (2007–2008). He is currently a member of the Sigma Lambda Chapter of Alpha Phi Alpha Fraternity, Inc.
