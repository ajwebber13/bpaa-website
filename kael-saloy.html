Kael Saloy

Board Treasurer

Kael Saloy is a Fall 2006 initiate of the Beta Phi Chapter of Alpha Phi Alpha Fraternity, Inc. Kael serves as Chief Financial Officer of ReNew Schools. He earned a degree in Economics and Finance from Dillard University in 2008.

He was the first elected Mister Dillard University (2007–2008), demonstrating early leadership and campus engagement.

He currently serves within the Sigma Lambda Chapter of Alpha Phi Alpha Fraternity, Inc.
