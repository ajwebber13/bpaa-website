Biography

Brother Montae Richardson is a Fall 2006 initiate of the Beta Phi Chapter of Alpha Phi Alpha Fraternity, Inc. He serves as a senior IT project manager with a background in computer science and systems leadership. He earned his Bachelor of Science in Computer Science and was the first Mister Dillard University (2006–2007). He is an active member of the Beta Lambda Chapter of Alpha Phi Alpha Fraternity, Inc. Outside of his professional work, he is a husband and father committed to leadership, technology, and service.
