Montae Richardson

Board Member

Montae Richardson is a Fall 2006 initiate of the Beta Phi Chapter of Alpha Phi Alpha Fraternity Inc. Montae is a Senior IT Project Manager with a background in Computer Science and enterprise systems.

He was the first Mister Dillard University (2006–2007), showing early leadership and engagement.

He is an active member of the Beta Lambda Chapter and continues to serve in professional and fraternity leadership roles.

He is also a husband and father balancing family, career, and service.
